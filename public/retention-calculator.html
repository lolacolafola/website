How we calculate
We model what happens to your revenue at your current vs potential retention rate over your chosen time horizon. Each percentage point of improvement means more customers completing year 2 and year 3 — and the gap between current and potential widens the longer the time horizon, because you're retaining a larger base each year.
These figures are estimates based on your inputs. Actual results will vary.
